# AI Agent BOB Technical Specification - Part 4

## The Broader Context: Virtuals Protocol & Monetization
Bob is not just a bot; it's an economic entity on the **Virtuals Protocol** (Base network).

### Economic Model
- **Inference Services:** Interaction fees for recommendations generate revenue.
- **Buyback and Burn:** Revenue used to buy $AGENT tokens, reducing supply.
- **Autonomous Trading:** Potential for BOB to trade vaulted assets independently.

### Security & Governance
- **Character Cards:** The "system prompt" and personality are encoded. Security of these cards is critical (recent $10k bounty for vulnerabilities).
- **Brand Engagement:** "My Bar" data allows brands (like Campari) to target loyal collectors for exclusive events/selections.

### Future Capabilities
- **AI Blending:** Integrating insights from AI-driven whiskey production (e.g., Mackmyra) into consumer recommendations.
- **Portfolio Management:** Transitioning from passive advisor to active agentic trade facilitator.
